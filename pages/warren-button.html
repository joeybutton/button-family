Warren A. Button

November 4, 1931 – April 15, 2022

Father, provider, craftsman

About

Warren Arthur Button was born in Middlesex, New York. He earned his undergraduate degree in animal husbandry from Cornell University, a master’s in Agricultural Economics from Penn State, and an MBA from Harvard Business School.

He served three years as a United States Navy officer, including an Arctic deployment. His business career spanned corporate development at Freeport Sulphur and culminated as Chairman and CEO of Able Industries, from which he retired in 2008.

Warren and Patricia Keller Button were married for 69 years. Together they raised five children—Alan, Richard, James, Christine, and Thomas—and were blessed with 17 grandchildren and 5 great-grandchildren.

A devoted member of his community, Warren served as a Boy Scout leader for Montrose Troop 36, a Sunday School teacher, and president of the Hendrick Hudson School Board. His passions ranged from woodworking and fossil collecting to singing, playing piano, hiking the Appalachian Trail, and studying astronomy.
